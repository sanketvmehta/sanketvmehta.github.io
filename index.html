Sanket Vaibhav Mehta

I'm a PhD student at the Language Technologies Institute (LTI) at School of Computer Science, Carnegie Mellon University. I'm advised by Jaime Carbonell and Barnabás Póczos and work on continual lifelong learning.

Previously, I obtained a Master's degree (2019) from the LTI.

Prior to joining CMU, I worked as a member of research staff at Big Data Research Lab, Adobe Research (2015-17) where I worked on interesting and challenging research problems in the domain of Digital Marketing.

I did my bachelors in Computer Science and Engineering (2011-15) from Indian Institute of Technology Roorkee.
